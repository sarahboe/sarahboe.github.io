FASHION FASHION FASHION



I have always been captivated by the clothes but especially by the people.

I like to observe how the people around me gets dressed and why they make these choices.
I like the diversity of shapes and colors and it is fascinating to see that we can do as many things with a wardrobe that remains more or less the same... dress, top, pants, jacket... all this, is fascinating to me.
So it was hollowing for me to want to study it, to devote myself to it... to make it my job.

Here is the beginning of my travels, which will be long... I hope !

My brand clothing, my baby: YUNKA. YUNKA is a designer clothing brand for women. SUMMER 15 is a collection primarily inspired by the Japanese style shapes and the work around dyes. Many clothes are inspired by the shibori and tie and dye techniques. Each part of the collection is unique and could be reworked according to commands for colors and patterns.
